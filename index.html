Rock Stars

Jerry Garcia

Jerome John Garcia (August 1, 1942 - August 9, 1995) was an American musician known for being the principal songwriter, lead guitarist, and a vocalist with the rock band Grateful Dead.

Tina Turner

Tina Turner (born Anna Mae Bullock; November 26, 1939 - May 24, 2023) was a singer, songwriter and actress. Known as the "Queen of Rock 'n' Roll"...

Frank Zappa

Frank Vincent Zappa (December 21, 1940 - December 4, 1993) was an American musician, composer, and bandleader known for experimentation and satire of American culture.

Dolores O'Riordan

Dolores Mary Eileen O'Riordan (6 September 1971 – 15 January 2018) was the lead vocalist and lyricist of alternative rock band The Cranberries.

Gwen Stefani

Gwen Renée Stefani (born October 3, 1969) is an American singer, songwriter, and lead vocalist of No Doubt. Known for hits like "Just a Girl" and "Don't Speak".

Elvis Presley

Elvis Aaron Presley (January 8, 1935 - August 16, 1977) was an American singer and actor known as the "King of Rock and Roll." A cultural icon of the 20th century.
